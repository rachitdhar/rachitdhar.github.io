About

Hi, I am Rachit (@rachitdhar). Here I am planning to share all sorts of interesting stuff that I have built or written. I have worked on various programming languages and several different kinds of software (including console apps, desktop tools, web, games, libraries), and really like exploring new and creative ideas that I often work on recreationally; with the side effect that they occassionally happen to also be useful. Personally, I like thinking from a fundamentals approach, and taking care of small but important low-level technical details that matter for performance: including memory, algorithmic time complexity, and overall system design.

Besides this, my interests are quite broad — ranging from areas like physics, mathematics, computer science and engineering, to philosophy, evolutionary biology, psychology, art, music, chess, and classic literary works. Of course, given the breadth of it all, I wouldn't claim to know all subjects to a significant depth; but I hope to keep learning!
